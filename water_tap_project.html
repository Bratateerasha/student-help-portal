Automatic Water Tap

Aim

To save water by allowing water flow only when needed.

Materials Required

  • Battery
  • IR Sensor / Transistor
  • Tap or Pipe Model
  • Connecting Wires

Working

When a hand or glass comes near the tap, the sensor detects it and allows water to flow automatically. When the object is removed, the water stops.

Advantages

  • Saves water
  • Easy to use
  • Low cost

Uses

Used in schools, hospitals, homes, and public places.
