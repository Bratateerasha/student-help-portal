Rainwater Harvesting

Aim

To collect and store rainwater for future use.

Materials Required

  • Plastic bottles
  • Pipes
  • Water tank
  • Cardboard base

Working

Rainwater from rooftops flows through pipes and is collected in a storage tank for reuse.

Advantages

  • Saves water
  • Reduces water shortage
  • Eco-friendly

Uses

Used in homes, schools, and buildings.
